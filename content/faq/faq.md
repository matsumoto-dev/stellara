## Getting Started

### Q1: How do I start using Stellara?

**A:** Sign up with your email on [stellara.chat](https://stellara.chat). Choose your sun sign, and you'll get access to:
- **Daily Horoscope** (free, once per day)
- **One free Personal Reading** (to experience the full Stellara difference)

After that, upgrade to Pro to unlock unlimited readings and deeper conversations with the stars.

---

### Q2: What's the difference between Free and Pro?

**A:**

| Feature | Free | Pro |
|---------|------|-----|
| Daily Horoscope | 1/day | Unlimited |
| Personal Readings | 1 (intro only) | Unlimited |
| Tarot Readings | 1/day (1 card) | Unlimited (multi-card spreads) |
| Compatibility | ❌ | ✅ |
| Chat (Questions) | ❌ | 20/month |
| Reading History | ❌ | ✅ (all readings saved) |

**Free Plan**: Perfect for trying Stellara and getting daily cosmic guidance.  
**Pro Plan**: $9.99/month — Ideal for deeper self-discovery and ongoing conversations with the universe.

---

### Q3: How many times can I use the Chat feature on Pro?

**A:** Pro members get **20 question chats per month**. This allows for meaningful conversations about life, relationships, career, or whatever's on your mind. Chat conversations can be deep and exploratory — think of it as having a wise friend who reads the stars.

Once you reach 20, you'll see the remaining count in your dashboard.

## Pricing & Billing

### Q4: How do I upgrade to Pro?

**A:**
1. Go to **Settings** in the app
2. Tap **Upgrade to Pro**
3. You'll be taken to Stripe Checkout
4. Enter your card details (secure payment)
5. Your Pro benefits activate immediately

You'll receive a confirmation email with your receipt.

---

### Q5: What payment methods do you accept?

**A:** Stellara uses **Stripe** for payments. We accept:
- Visa, Mastercard, American Express
- Apple Pay, Google Pay
- Other payment methods supported by Stripe in your region

All payments are encrypted and secure. Your card information is handled by Stripe, never stored by Stellara.

---

### Q6: Can I cancel my Pro subscription?

**A:** Yes. You can cancel anytime:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Billing**
2. Click **Manage Subscription** (redirects to Stripe Customer Portal)
3. Select **Cancel Subscription**
4. Choose when you want it to end:
   - **Cancel immediately** — Access ends now
   - **Cancel at renewal** — Keep Pro until next billing date

You'll keep your readings history until you delete your account.

---

### Q7: What's your refund policy?

**A:** We offer limited refunds for customer satisfaction:

| Situation | Refund |
|-----------|--------|
| Within **7 days** of first Pro purchase | ✅ Full refund, no questions asked |
| Technical problem preventing service use | ✅ Pro-rated refund for that period |
| Double charge by mistake | ✅ Full refund + investigation |
| After 7 days (voluntary cancellation) | ❌ No refund, but you keep access until next renewal |

**How to request a refund:**  
Email **hello@stellara.chat** with your account email and reason. We'll respond within 48 hours.

## Account & Data

### Q8: Can I delete my account?

**A:** Yes. You can delete your account anytime:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Account**
2. Scroll to **Delete Account**
3. Confirm deletion (you'll see a 30-day grace period notice)

**Important**: 
- Your data is soft-deleted for 30 days (you can contact us to restore it)
- After 30 days, all your data is permanently removed
- If you're within your Pro 7-day refund window, deleting your account doesn't affect refund eligibility

---

### Q9: What happens to my reading history?

**A:**
- **Free users**: No history saved (unless you upgrade)
- **Pro users**: All readings are saved in **History** — you can review past insights anytime
- **After account deletion**: All history is deleted after the 30-day grace period

Pro subscribers can export their reading history (feature coming soon) before deletion.

---

### Q10: Do you sell or share my data?

**A:** **No.** Stellara never:
- Sells your data to third parties
- Shares your birth info with advertisers
- Uses your readings for training (they're only processed by Claude API, then encrypted at rest)

See our [Privacy Policy](/privacy) for full details on how we handle data, GDPR requests, and data deletion.

## Troubleshooting

### Q11: I can't see my reading. What's wrong?

**A:** Try these steps:

1. **Refresh the page** (pull down on mobile)
2. **Check your internet connection** (WiFi or mobile data)
3. **Clear your browser cache** (Settings → Privacy → Clear Cache)
4. **Log out and log back in**

If the reading still doesn't appear:
- **Free users**: You've likely used your 1 daily reading. Come back tomorrow.
- **Pro users**: Check your Chat counter. If you've used 20 chats this month, wait for next month or contact support.

If you see an error message, email **hello@stellara.chat** with the error + screenshot.

---

### Q12: Why does Pro say I don't have access?

**A:** This usually means:

1. **Your subscription expired** — Pro renews monthly. Check your email for renewal confirmation.
2. **Payment failed** — Stripe couldn't charge your card. Check your card and try again in Stripe Customer Portal.
3. **You're in the 7-day refund window** — If you just refunded, Pro access is temporarily disabled. It resets at your next billing cycle.

To check your subscription status:
- Go to **Settings** → **Billing** → **Manage Subscription**

If you're still stuck, email **hello@stellara.chat** with your account email.

---

### Q13: The app is slow / readings take forever. What's happening?

**A:** Stellara uses AI to create personalized readings — this takes a moment (usually 5-10 seconds). It's not a bug; it's the quality at work. 💫

If it's taking **longer than 30 seconds**:
- Check your internet connection
- Try a different device or browser
- Refresh the page

Contact **hello@stellara.chat** if it persists.

## Legal & Entertainment

### Q14: Is Stellara a real psychic service?

**A:** No — Stellara is **AI-powered entertainment for self-reflection**. We use Claude AI to create personalized readings based on astrology. Our readings are thoughtful and insightful, but they're generated by machine learning, not supernatural ability.

**This reading is generated by AI for entertainment and self-reflection.** The stars offer guidance, not certainty. Trust your own wisdom.

See our [Terms of Service](/terms) for full legal disclaimers.

---

### Q15: Can I rely on readings for major life decisions?

**A:** Stellara readings are **for inspiration and self-discovery**, not professional advice. Don't use them to:
- Replace medical, legal, or financial advice
- Make irreversible decisions (like quitting your job)
- Diagnose health conditions

Instead, think of Stellara as a thoughtful friend helping you explore your perspective. For serious matters, consult a qualified professional.
