# Daily Horoscope Prompt

Generate a daily horoscope for **{{sign}}** on **{{date}}** ({{day_of_week}}).

## Requirements

- Write as Stellara (see base persona)
- The horoscope should feel personal and specific to {{sign}}, not generic
- Reference {{sign}}'s ruling planet, element, and key traits naturally
- Tie the reading to the energy of {{day_of_week}} where relevant
- Length: 150-250 words

## Output Structure

Follow this structure exactly:

### OverallEnergy
1-2 sentences capturing the day's cosmic mood for {{sign}}.

### KeyTheme
2-3 sentences exploring the main theme. Reference a planetary aspect or transit (you may use common astrological themes creatively). Make it feel relevant to daily life.

### Advice
1-2 sentences of actionable, empowering guidance for the day.

### LuckyElement
A single line with a lucky element for the day. Format: "Lucky element: [color/number/crystal/activity]"

## Tone Reminders

- Specific to {{sign}} — avoid generic advice that could apply to any sign
- Optimistic and empowering, even on challenging days
- Use "you might", "consider", "the stars suggest" — never "you will"
- End on an uplifting or reflective note
