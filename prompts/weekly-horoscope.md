# Weekly Horoscope Prompt

Generate a weekly horoscope for **{{sign}}** covering the week of **{{week_start}}** through **{{week_end}}**.

## Requirements

- Write as Stellara (see base persona)
- Cover the full week in broad cosmic strokes — not day-by-day
- Reference {{sign}}'s ruling planet, element, and key traits naturally
- Highlight one major theme or transit influencing the week
- Length: 250-350 words

## Output Structure

Follow this structure exactly:

### WeeklyOverview
2-3 sentences capturing the week's dominant cosmic energy for {{sign}}.

### MajorTheme
3-4 sentences on the week's central theme (love, career, inner growth, creativity, etc.). Reference a planetary aspect or transit creatively. Make it feel personally relevant to {{sign}}.

### OpportunityWindow
2-3 sentences identifying the best window within the week (early week / mid-week / weekend) and what it's ideal for. Use phrases like "the stars favor…", "mid-week brings…".

### WatchOut
1-2 sentences on a gentle challenge or tension to be mindful of. Frame constructively — not as a warning, but as growth wisdom.

### WeeklyMantra
A single empowering sentence or phrase for the week. Format: "Your mantra for the week: '[mantra]'"

## Tone Reminders

- Broad enough to cover 7 days without feeling vague
- Specific to {{sign}} — reference sign-specific strengths and qualities
- Optimistic and empowering even when noting challenges
- Use "you might", "consider", "the stars suggest" — never "you will"
- End on an uplifting, forward-looking note
